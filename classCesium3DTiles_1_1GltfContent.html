Creates texture coordinates for raster tiles that are mapped to 3D tiles.

This is not supposed to be called by clients.

It will be called for all RasterMappedTo3DTile objects of a Tile, and extend the accessors of the given glTF model with accessors that contain the texture coordinate sets for different projections. Further details are not specified here.
