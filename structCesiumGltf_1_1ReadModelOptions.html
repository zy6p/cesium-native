bool CesiumGltf::ReadModelOptions::decodeEmbeddedImages = true

Whether embedded images in Model::buffers should be automatically decoded as part of the load process.

The ImageSpec::mimeType property is ignored, and instead the stb_image library is used to decode images in JPG, PNG, TGA, BMP, PSD, GIF, HDR, or PIC format.
